About Our Project and The Team

About the Project

T.A.L.O.S. (Teaching Assistant for Learning Optimization & Success) is designed to enhance learning at BYU-Idaho by providing a dedicated Teaching Assistant/Tutor Chatbot. Our goal is to develop a specialized LLM that students can access 24/7, tailored specifically to their course, instructor, and subject matter. By training the model on relevant materials, T.A.L.O.S. will serve as an expert in subjects such as Statistics, Biology, or Python, offering accurate and course-specific assistanceâ€”all while maintaining the highest quality of education.
